Accessibility Statement

MORTIS (Mapped Open-Route Threat Identification System) is committed to making this application usable by as many people as possible, including those who rely on assistive technologies such as screen readers, keyboard navigation, and browser zoom.

Conformance status

This prototype aims to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA. As a prototype under active development it may not yet fully satisfy every success criterion, and we welcome reports of any barriers you encounter.

Measures we have taken

The following measures are built into the application:

  • Semantic HTML (native buttons, form controls, headings, tables and landmarks).
  • Form fields with explicit, programmatically associated labels.
  • A colour palette chosen to meet the 4.5:1 minimum text contrast ratio.
  • Clearly visible keyboard focus indicators on all interactive controls.
  • A "Skip to main content" link for keyboard and screen-reader users.
  • A scan progress bar exposed to assistive technology via ARIA.
  • Content that reflows and remains usable at up to 200% zoom.

Reporting an accessibility problem

If you find any part of this application difficult to use, please let us know so we can put it right. You can report accessibility issues by reprting them through the github repository at https://github.com/hs7277OU/M.O.R.T.I.S_App/issues.
